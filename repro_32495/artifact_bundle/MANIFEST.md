# Manifest

- `code/`: reproduction, result-ingestion, figure, bundle, and K-LIVE audit scripts.
- `outputs/`: machine-readable GPU trials/details/summary, K-LIVE audit JSON, figures, and Job logs.
- `source/`: the publicly linked paper PDF/text and a note on source availability.
- `poster/`: Posterly HTML, embedded logbook figure, preview PDF/PNG, and gate report.
- `README.md`: scope and download guidance.

No credentials, tokens, environment files, virtual environments, or caches are included.
